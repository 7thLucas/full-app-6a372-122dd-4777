# ForgeRealms — Design Guidelines

## Color Palette
- Primary dark: #1e2a4a (deep navy — main backgrounds, headings)
- Accent: #f97316 (fiery orange — CTAs, highlights, energy)
- Deep dark: #0f172a (near-black — hero sections, visual panels)
- Surface light: #f8fafc (off-white card backgrounds)
- Border: #e2e8f0 (subtle card borders)
- Accent warm: #fff7ed with #fed7aa border (warm highlights)
- Text muted: #64748b, #94a3b8

## Typography
- Headings: Font-black (900 weight), tight line-height (1.0–1.1)
- Subheadings: Font-bold (700), 1.3–1.4 line-height
- Body: Regular weight, 1.5–1.6 line-height, slate tones
- Labels/tags: Uppercase, extra-wide letter-spacing, xs size, font-bold

## Visual Style
- Epic and cinematic — this is a game platform, not a SaaS dashboard
- Dark hero panels with glowing orange accents
- Card-based layouts with generous padding (p-5 to p-7)
- Rounded corners: rounded-2xl throughout
- Strategy game aesthetic: maps, kingdoms, units, forge/anvil imagery
- AI-powered feel: generative, dynamic, powerful

## Components
- Primary CTA: Dark navy background (#1e2a4a) with orange text or orange background with white text
- Cards: Light surface (#f8fafc) with subtle border, or warm (#fff7ed) with orange border for highlights
- Tags/chips: Rounded-full, small, color-coded by category
- Images: Full-bleed within visual panels, slight opacity overlay for text readability

## Elevation & Spacing
- Section padding: p-10 to p-12
- Card padding: p-5 to p-7
- Gap between cards: gap-3 to gap-4
- Rounded corners: rounded-2xl (cards), rounded-xl (inner elements), rounded-full (chips)

## Anti-references
- No generic SaaS blue/purple
- No flat minimal design — this needs weight and drama
- No light/airy aesthetic — the platform should feel powerful and epic
